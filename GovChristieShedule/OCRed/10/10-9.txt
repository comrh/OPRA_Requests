 Governor Chris Christie To Host 130th Town Hall Next Tuesday In Somerset County    For Immediate Release                                                                Contact: Kevin RobertsThursday, March 5, 2015                                                                             609-777-2600 
Trenton, NJ Å’ Next Tuesday, Governor Chris Christie will travel to Somerville to host his 130th Town Hall Meeting. Governor Christie will speak about his proposed budget and reform initiatives for New Jersey, in addition to answering questions from members of the audience.   Residents can RSVP to TownHall.Somerville@nj.gov. Doors open at 9:30 a.m. and seating is on a first come, first serve basis and open to the public. No bags of any kind are permitted in the venue and personal items are subject to search. Due to safety requirements, law enforcement officers will be in attendance and will wand all attendees.
