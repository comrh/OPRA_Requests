 Governor Chris Christie To Host 115th Town Hall Thursday In Hunterdon County Governor Christie Will Discuss Fiscal Year 2015 Budget And Making The Choices That Will Lead To A Better New Jersey For Immediate Release                                                                Contact: Michael Drewniak Monday, March 17, 2014                                                                                    Colin Reed                                                                                                                       609-777-2600  
Trenton, NJ ŒGovernor Chris Christie will travel to Flemington on Thursday, March 20 to host his 115th Town Hall Meeting. Governor Christie will discuss his Fiscal Year 2015 Budget and making the choice to not sacrifice the priorities that matter most to our state on the altar of exploding entitlement costs Œ pension, health benefits, and debt service.  Residents can RSVP to TownHall.Hunterdon@gov.state.nj.us. Doors open at 9:00 am and seating is on a first come, first serve basis and open to the public. No bags of any kind are permitted in the venue and personal items are subject to search. Due to safety requirements, law enforcement will be in attendance and will wand all attendees.
